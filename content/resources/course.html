RStudio

  • Choose one of two options to use RStudio:
    • Log on to the Smith College RStudio Server.
    • Use RStudio locally:
      • Download and instal RStudio Desktop
      • Download and install the following packages: install.packages=c("Stat2Data","tidyverse", "mosaic")
